Hotel Benco

About Hotel Benco

Featuring a bar and a terrace, Hotel Benco is located in Namasuba. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi. There is free private parking and the property has paid airport shuttle service.

The rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Hotel Benco provides some accommodations with lake views, and rooms are equipped with a balcony. Guest rooms have a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.
